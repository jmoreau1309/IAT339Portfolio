Project Goal

This final project for my graphic design class was done in groups of four and gave the teams a designer precedent and client to use to create a microsite. Using our Designer Precedent, Wolfgang Weingart, we created advertising posters for our client, the Holland Festival, using Weingart's design principles. The poster designs would then be used to inspire our microsite, which would primarily be a space for users to view shows and purchase tickets.

Client Analysis

Our designer precedent is Wolfgang Weingart is known as the father of New Wave Typography. He was a typesetter and his work revolved around the use of typographic images, photomontage, layering textures, and color. The Holland Festival exhibits artists in order to facilitate collaborations and encourage the meeting of creative minds. It is an international performing arts festival but they also show other arts like visual arts and photography.
